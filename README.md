# How to make the emotions:
Contempt: Raise one side of your mouth up into a half smile\
Happiness: Regular smile (raise both sides of your mouth up)\
Sadness: Squish your eyebrows together and make a frown\
Anger: Squish your eyebrows down in anger like Angry Birds and open your eyes as well as tighten your mouth\
Fear: Anger + jaw opening, so squish eyebrows down, eyes open, jaw open but stretch it sideways as well\
Surprise: Raise your eyebrows and open your jaw slightly\
Disgust: Scrunch your nose and droop your mouth like a disgusted face
